Such motions are distinct from points of order 

see Reglamento art. 114 for typified motions

One (/cuestionamiento al orador/) to interrogate the speaker, who must also accept the question be made. 

Another is (/alusiones personales/), to give right of reply to alluded members by recognizing them immediately after the speaker ends. 

And (/rectificación de hechos/) wind up an additional name at the end of the list of speakers.
